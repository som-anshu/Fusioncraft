# Fusioncraft

A collection of 3D modeling components, assemblies, and rapid prototyping designs developed during the Fusioncraft Autodesk Fusion 360 training program.

---

## Features

- **Parametric Modeling**: Sketches utilizing geometric and dimensional constraints for easy scalability.
- **Assembly Design**: Multi-component assemblies featuring proper mechanical joints and motion links to simulate real-world motion.
- **Optimization for 3D Printing**: Part tolerances and geometries tailored specifically for additive manufacturing (FDM/SLA).

---

## Tools & Workspace Environment

- **Software**: Autodesk Fusion 360
- **Target Manufacturing**: Rapid prototyping / 3D Printing (Bambu Studio / Cura)
